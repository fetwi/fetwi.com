Make cookies insh'Allah!

It takes like 30 mins. You'll need stand mixer or strong arms.

Ingredients
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoons table salt
  • 2 teaspoons or a packet of vanilla sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 360 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted room temperature butter
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

1
Whisk
  • Whisk together eggs, brown and white sugar, salt and vanilla.
    • On a mixer: Set it on high speed for 6 mins.
    • By hand: Make dua and whisk for 20mins.

    Note: Whisk until it turns light and fluffy. You can also preheat the oven to 350°F now.

2
Mix
  • Mix in baking soda and flour then butter until it is just blended.
  • Lightly fold in the chocolate chips.

    • Note: Save some chocolate chips to put on top of cookies before baking.

3
Bake
  • Put in prheated oven set to 350°F.
  • Spoon on cookie dough on a parchment lined sheet pan.
  • Put cookies on the top rack and bake for 6 mins.
  • Broil at the end of bake for 30 secs.
    • Note: Careful, it gets dark quick like Warsame (Navy blue and white).
  • Let cool on wire rack for 10 mins.
